About Us

About Ana

Ana was born in South Florida and lived there until she graduated high school. She then attended Georgia Tech for college where she then called Atlanta her home for the next four years. While attending there, she made many friends, joined Delta Phi Lambda Sorority, Inc. and met Nicholas during her junior year in college. Ana graduated with a B.S. in Industrial Engineering in 2012 and now resides in Stamford, CT with her fiancé Nicholas and their corgi named Luna.

About Nick

Nicholas was born in Orlando, Florida and lived there until the end of 1996 when he moved to the Atlanta area of Georgia. After four years of high school he attended Georgia Tech and made friends while being a teaching assistant in the college of computing. Through one of these friends he met Ana in October of 2010. Nicholas graduated with a B.S. in Applied Mathematics in 2012 and lives in Stamford, CT with his fiancée Ana and their dog Luna.

How We Met

Ana and Nick met in October 2010. Ana would get coffee with her friend Ryan some mornings before class when one day, he brought his friend Nick to join them for coffee as well. It turns out Nick's birthday was coming up and Ana loved any excuse to bake something for her friends so she decides to bake Nick a cupcake even though they only knew each other for a week at that point. Ana and Nick would then hang out in the Tech Rec between classes. They enjoyed playing billiards there and playing late night card games with their friends and became very close. Seven months later in May 2011 Nick and Ana officially started dating and have been together ever since.
